Lean Improvement Platform

This platform is designed for internal process improvement. Each week, on-site team members submit improvement suggestions. The focal person reviews and approves them. Once approved, reports are recorded and can be viewed by administrators, senior managers, and directors.

Workflow

  • Site team submits weekly improvement report.
  • Focal person reviews and approves or requests modifications.
  • Approved reports are stored in the database.
  • Admins can view all submissions and track progress.
  • Senior managers and directors can view summaries and analytics.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Backend: Python (Flask / Django)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Additional: AJAX for asynchronous data requests
